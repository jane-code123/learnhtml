Puerto Princesa

Puerto Princesa is the capital and major flight gateway in Palawan. If you’re visiting El Nido, then Puerto Princesa, “The City in the Forest,” is worth a stopover.
Start with a trip to Puerto Princesa Underground River, the 2nd longest navigable underground river in the world, listed as a World Heritage Site by UNESCO and New7Wonders of Nature.
Then, spend a day beach bumming and snorkeling on an island hopping tour around Honda Bay.
Don’t leave without going on the city tour and feasting on fresh seafood.
